Recent News

Recent news in Bangladesh has been dominated by political tensions, with the government revoking the passport of ousted Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina and seeking her extradition from India. This follows violent protests in July that led to Hasina fleeing the country. Meanwhile, ties with India remain strained, with Bangladesh canceling judicial training sessions for its officials in India and the two countries' border forces holding a flag meeting amid ongoing border disputes. Economically, Bangladesh is experiencing increased pressure on its ports, with a record number of foreign ships waiting to dock.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh launched its first satellite, the Bangabandhu Satellite-1, on May 12, 2018. The satellite was launched from the Kennedy Space Center in the United States using a SpaceX Falcon 9 Block 5 rocket. This made Bangladesh the 57th country to independently operate a satellite in space.

Ensure safety of our winter guests

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

The gradual decline of migratory bird population in our water bodies, such as haors, beels and lakes, demonstrates our failure as a host. Every winter, these birds travel thousands of kilometres to escape the biting cold of their homelands for Bangladesh's warm tropical waters. In recent years, instead of finding a safe and welcoming sanctuary, these waterfowls—especially those coming to Hakaluki Haor in Moulvibazar and Sylhet—have been subjected to rampant poaching, noise, and plastic pollution, according to a report by The Daily Star.
